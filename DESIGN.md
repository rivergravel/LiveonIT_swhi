# Design System Theme

## Color Palette

Our color palette is derived from a `light` color mode, centered around a primary seed color of `#004071`. We utilize a `fidelity` variant, aiming for a consistent and trustworthy aesthetic.

* **Primary Color:** `#004071` - The brand's most distinctive chromatic color, suitable for buttons, CTAs, and key interactive elements.
* **Secondary Color:** `#536070` - A supporting color for less prominent UI elements, chips, and secondary actions.
* **Tertiary Color:** `#2E7D32` - An additional accent color for highlights, badges, or decorative elements.
* **Neutral Color:** `#F8F9FA` - A neutral base color for backgrounds, surfaces, and non-chromatic elements.

## Typography

The typography is chosen for readability and a modern feel, creating a clear visual hierarchy.

* **Headlines:** `publicSans` - Used for all major headings and titles.
* **Body Text:** `publicSans` - Applied to paragraphs, descriptions, and general text content.
* **Labels:** `inter` - Employed for UI labels, form fields, and smaller textual elements requiring distinct clarity.

## Shape and Spacing

Our design maintains a balance between form and function.

* **Roundedness:** `1` (Subtle roundedness) - Corners of UI shapes feature a subtle rounding, offering a friendly yet defined appearance.
* **Spacing:** `2` (Normal) - We adhere to a normal level of whitespace, providing adequate separation between elements for comfortable readability and visual balance.
